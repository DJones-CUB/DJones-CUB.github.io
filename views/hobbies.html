Hobbies & Interests

Game Development

A long time hobby of mine, I hope to (one day) pursue game development as an independent developer. I've always enjoyed the development process, even as a frusturated kid trying to understand how namespaces work in C++, and design/development is something I could see myself doing for the rest of my life. My ultimate hope is to one day work for Valve Software, known most notably for Steam, the Source Game Engine, and their litanny of genre-spanning and ground-breaking games.

Miniature Painting

A relatively recent hobby, I got into Warhammer 40k (or Warhammer 40,000) some half a decade ago, but only picked up the trademark of the franchise, miniature painting, a few months ago. I've always enjoyed art and the artistic process, but I've never been able to effectively draw or paint due to shaky hands, but I found that miniature painting, despite the frustrations and the expense, has been one of the most calming and peaceful hobbies that I've picked up over the years.

Music

Somewhere between game development and miniature painting is music. My mother was a classical bassoonist and got me into playing music at a very young age. I (used to) play the Violin, I can play a small amount of piano, and have taught myself how to play guitar. My first guitar was my first real purchase, only costing about $400, which has lasted me since freshman year of highschool without a single string snapping or any notable damage to the guitar itself.
